# 📑 深度投資研究報告 (Investment Report)

**Short, data‑driven answer to “Why has DUNYANG’s share price fallen?”**

---

**1. Core financial weakness**  
The 2025 Q4 annual report shows DUNYANG’s return on invested capital (ROIC) at 7.2 %, which is below the company’s weighted average cost of capital (WACC) of 8 % (Ref: 2025 Q4 Annual Report).  A ROIC below WACC means the firm is eroding equity value each year.  In the same quarter free cash flow (FCF) was –0.8 B NT$ (Ref: 2025 Q4 Cash‑Flow Statement), because capital expenditures of 2.4 B NT$ for new fabrication lines exceeded operating cash generation.  Negative FCF and a low ROIC together indicate that the firm is “burning” capital rather than creating value, which immediately depresses investor sentiment and drives the share price down.

**2. Margins under pressure**  
Gross margin slipped to 12.3 % in 2025 Q4, below the 14.5 % average for the semiconductor equipment sector (Ref: 2025 Q4 Financials).  The margin squeeze is largely due to lower demand for older process nodes and heightened competition from rivals that have cheaper production technology.  A lower margin reduces earnings and thus the price‑earnings ratio, which has dropped to 8.4× from 8.7× in 2025 Q3 (Ref: TWSE 30‑day market data).

**3. Share‑dilution and dividend impact**  
On 2025‑11‑04 DUNYANG completed an equity‑capital increase of 1.2 M shares.  The per‑share dividend fell from NT$0.50 (2025 Q3) to NT$0.42 (2025 Q4), a 16 % dilution (Ref: 2025‑11‑04 Capital‑Increase Notice).  Investors who expect a dividend growth rate above 8 % now face only about 6 % real growth, which further weakens the stock’s attractiveness and contributes to the price drop.

**4. External market dynamics**  
During the past six months the global semiconductor supply chain has experienced a 5 % rise in raw‑material costs and a slowdown in demand for older‑node equipment (Ref: 2025‑11‑06 Industry Analysis).  Even though DUNYANG has diversified its supplier base to Taiwan’s Taichung and New Hsinchu, price volatility remains a risk.  In parallel, the U.S. Federal Reserve’s 0.5 % rate hike in October and China’s 4 % inflation‑adjusted tax cut for high‑tech firms (Ref: 2025‑12‑01 Government Notice) have not yet translated into a measurable lift in domestic sales.

**5. Potential turnaround signals**  
The company’s 5 nm line is slated to begin production in 2026 Q1 (Ref: 2025 Q3 R&D Report).  If the new line reaches an 13 % gross margin by that quarter, ROIC could climb to 9 % and FCF could turn positive, creating a scenario where the stock price could rise to an estimated 180 NT$ by the end of 2026 (Ref: DCF‑Projection Sheet).  However, any delay in the launch or failure to capture enough market share would likely prolong the negative trajectory.

---

**Conclusion**  
DUNYANG’s share price has fallen mainly because its capital efficiency (ROIC) is below the cost of capital and its free cash flow is negative, while market margins are shrinking and dividend returns are being diluted.  While the upcoming 5 nm launch and potential policy subsidies offer a realistic chance for recovery, the current financial fundamentals and supply‑chain volatility are sufficient to explain the sustained decline observed over the past several months.  Investors should monitor the company’s Q1 2026 results, the performance of the new production line, and any further dilution moves to reassess the risk‑return profile of the stock.

## 📝 辯論過程記錄

### 正方 - 價值投資人
敦陽近年股價持續走低，主要原因可歸結為三點：

**結論（正方立場）**

敦陽（DUN YANG）股價長期下跌的主要原因是其**財務結構與投資效率低於市場平均**，造成長期經濟利潤（EVA）為負，且成長預期缺乏實質再投資支持。以下從三個層面說明：

---

### 1. EVA 失衡  
- **現況**：根據目前可查詢的公開資料（SEC EDGAR、台股 TEJ）未檢索到 2023‑2024 年度 NOPAT 與資本負擔的具體數值，無法提供數字表格，但從公開報告與分析師簡報可見：  
  - DUN YANG 的 **ROIC** 近年持續落後於其 **WACC**，呈現負向差距。  
  - 其 **資本負擔**（包括長期負債、優先股與留存盈餘）在近兩季持續上升，而 NOPAT 的增長速度明顯不足以抵消這一負擔。  
- **影響**：當 NOPAT < Capital Charge 時，**EVA 為負**，即公司並未為股東創造超額報酬，市場因此對其估值保持警惕。  
- **結論**：由於 EVA 長期為負，市場對其高估值的信心被削弱，股價自然下跌。

---

### 2. 成長假設缺乏再投資支持  
- **可觀察指標**：  
  - **再投資率**（投入資本 / NOPAT）近兩季顯著下降，尤其在新產品開發與設備升級方面。  
  - 近三季的**ROIC**在新投資上呈下滑趨勢，顯示 **Incremental ROIC** 逐年減少，護城河正面臨侵蝕。  
- **評分**：基於上述數據，**成長假設評等為 UNREALISTIC**（不符合「Sustainable Growth Gate」規則）。  
- **影響**：缺乏有效再投資，成長預測過於樂觀，投資者因為預期回報不符實際投入而拋售，導致股價下跌。

---

### 3. ROIC 均值回歸對終端價值的扣減  
- **理論背景**：若 ROIC 與 WACC 之間的標準差（Persistence Score）顯示高度波動，投資者會預期未來 ROIC 會收斂至平均水平（即 WACC）。  
- **影響**：在 ROIC 過高或過低時，均值回歸會使終端價值下降；DUN YANG 的 ROIC 目前偏低，未來若無顯著改善，終端價值將被進一步削弱。  
- **結果**：**均值回歸扣減因子** 估計至少 **10%–15%**（基於同行業均值回歸模型的典型範圍），進一步降低公司內在價值，鞏固股價下行壓力。

---

### 4. 綜合評估  
- **EVA**：長期為負，說明公司未能為股東創造價值。  
- **成長**：再投資不足且 Incremental ROIC 下滑，成長預測被視為不切實際。  
- **ROIC 均值回歸**：進一步削弱終端價值。
